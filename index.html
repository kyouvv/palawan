Palawan is an archipelagic province of the Philippines, renowned for its stunning limestone cliffs, crystal-clear lagoons, and extraordinary marine biodiversity. Named the world's best island multiple times, it promises an unforgettable escape.

From the underground river of Puerto Princesa to the secret lagoons of El Nido and the wrecks of Coron — every corner tells a story.
